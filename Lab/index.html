Ultimate Two Ingredient Air Fry Catfish

Pressed for time? Craving a crispy, flavorful, and super quick meal? Win weeknight dinners or any meal with this seriously easy and delicious dish. Bye, boring dinners!

Ingredients:

  • 2 catfish fillets
  • Half a package of Louisiana Air Fryer Fish Mix

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your air fryer to 400°F (200°C) for about 5 minutes.
  2. In a bowl dredge the fillets in the Louisiana air fry mix, pressing gently to adhere the mix to the fish.
  3. Place the coated catfish fillets in the preheated air fryer basket, ensuring they are not overlapping.
  4. Cook the catfish in the air fryer for about 18 to 22 minutes, checking the crispiness halfway. NO NEED TO FLIP!
  5. Serve the air-fried catfish fillets with your favorite dipping sauce or alongside some tartar sauce, lemon wedges, or coleslaw.

Enjoy your crispy and delicious air-fried catfish!

Tips for Great Night Sleep

Tip 1

Limit screen time before bed.

Tip 2

Keep your bedroom cool, dark, and quiet.

Tip 3

Stick to a consistent sleep schedule, create a relaxing bedtime routine, and ensure a comfortable sleep environment.

Tip 4

Avoid stimulating activities before bed. Instead, opt for calm, relaxing activities that promote a sense of tranquility.

Tip 5

Consider using blackout curtains, earplugs, or white noise machines.

Tip 6

Read a book or taking a warm bath.
